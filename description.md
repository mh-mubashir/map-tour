France Colony, Islamabad Walls of Stigma: Commoning Infrastructures of Erasure

CAR MAINTENANCE PICTURE
TITLE: Skill Pooling
Cars park outside while repairs happen inside. A workshop room accessible from both the street and the settlement lets mechanics serve clients without either side needing to cross over.

WOMEN PROTESTING PICTURE
TITLE: Collective Bargaining
Residents negotiate service provision as a group rather than individually. Past protests at the boundary have successfully won electricity access and infrastructure concessions from the municipality.

DRIVERS REST AREA PICTURE
TITLE: Mutual Aid Workers
leaving early for long shifts have no place to wait, eat, or store belongings before the day starts. A room with seating, shade, and storage serves the daily needs of commuting residents.

CIRCULAR ECONOMY PICTURE
TITLE: Resource Redistribution
Discarded goods from F-7 households arrive as usable raw material. Residents receive, repair, and redistribute them, turning neighborhood surplus into household resource.

TAILOR'S WORKSHOP PICTURE
TITLE: Skill Sharing
Several women in the community tailor but work separately with no shared tools or space. A workshop lets them work together, share equipment, and take on larger orders collectively.

WATER TESTING PICTURE
TITLE: Environmental Stewardship
 Residents drawing water from the local well have no way to know whether it is safe. A testing station lets community members check and log water quality on a regular basis.

CHILDREN PAINTING THE MURAL PICTURE
TITLE: Spatial Appropriation
There is no open space in the settlement designated for gathering or play. Children use an open wall surface to paint a mural, marking the area as a shared community space.

RECYCLING ROOM PICTURE
TITLE: Gamified Resource Recovery
Waste is not sorted and builds up in shared drains and open areas. Children bring in collected trash and receive small rewards in return, making regular collection a habit.
